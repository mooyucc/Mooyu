"墨鱼足迹"用户隐私政策

更新日期：2025 年 11 月 11 日

一、引言

感谢您使用“墨鱼足迹”。我们非常重视您的个人信息和隐私保护。本《用户隐私政策》将向您说明我们如何收集、使用、存储、共享和保护您的个人信息，以及您可以如何管理这些信息。请在使用我们的产品或服务前，仔细阅读并充分理解本政策内容。若您不同意本政策的任何内容，请立即停止使用“墨鱼足迹”。

二、信息收集范围

我们遵循合法、正当、必要的原则，仅收集提供核心功能所需的最少信息：

  • Apple 登录信息：当您使用“使用 Apple 登录”时，我们根据您的授权获取 Apple 提供的必要信息（可能包含您的姓名、头像、Apple ID 关联邮箱或隐藏邮箱等），用于账号创建、登录验证与身份识别。
  • iCloud 数据：您授权后，我们在您的 iCloud 账户中写入并读取行程、目的地、备注等内容，帮助您跨设备同步数据。此类数据仅存放于您个人的 iCloud 空间。
  • 设备与操作信息：包括设备型号、操作系统、应用版本等，用于基本统计、性能优化与安全审计。
  • 使用日志与诊断信息：包括崩溃日志、错误记录等，仅用于改进产品体验和排查问题。

三、信息使用方式

我们将为以下目的处理您的个人信息：

  • 提供及维护核心功能，例如行程规划、目的地记录、路线管理等；
  • 保障服务安全，如身份验证、防作弊、防止非法使用；
  • 提供客服支持与反馈通道，回应您的咨询和建议；
  • 进行数据分析与研发，以提升产品性能与用户体验；
  • 在征得同意的前提下，为您推送通知、更新、活动或个性化内容；
  • 遵循法律法规与监管要求，如协助行政或司法机关依法执行。

我们不会将信息用于与上述目的无关的其他用途。

四、信息存储与保护

  • 存储地点与方式：除日志与必要运营信息由我们在中国境内安全存储外，您的行程等核心数据保存在您个人的 iCloud 账户中，不会另行存放于我们的自有服务器。我们仅在您授权的前提下读写这部分数据。
  • 存储期限：我们仅在实现上述目的所必需的期限内保留信息；在超出期限、服务终止或您主动删除/注销后，我们会对信息进行删除或匿名化处理（法律法规另有规定的除外）。存储在 iCloud 的数据可由您自行管理和删除。
  • 安全措施：我们采用传输与存储加密、访问控制、权限分级、审计监控等措施保护您信息的安全。仅授权人员可接触必要信息，并与其签署保密协议。
  • 安全事件处置：如发生信息安全事件，我们将按法律法规要求及时告知您事件情况、影响、采取的补救措施，并向主管部门上报。

五、信息共享、转让与公开披露

我们不会出售您的个人信息，也不会向 Apple 以外的第三方共享或转让您的信息。仅在以下情形下，可能进行共享或转让：

  • 取得您的明确授权或同意；
  • 根据法律法规、行政监管或司法要求；
  • 与 Apple 相关生态服务（例如 CloudKit、Sign in with Apple）的必要协作；这些服务均受 Apple 官方隐私政策约束；
  • 企业合并、重组等情况。如涉及信息转移，我们将要求新的信息持有者继续受本政策约束，或重新征得您的授权。

除法律另有规定外，我们不会公开披露您的个人信息。如确需公开披露，将以符合法律规定的方式提前告知并征得您的同意。

六、第三方服务与 SDK

“墨鱼足迹”目前仅使用 Apple 官方提供的 SDK，包括但不限于：

  • Sign in with Apple（用于身份认证）；
  • CloudKit / iCloud（用于数据存储与同步）。

这些服务受 Apple 相关隐私政策约束，建议您参阅 Apple Privacy Policy 以了解更多信息。

七、您的权利与信息管理

我们尊重您对个人信息的相关权利：

  • 访问与更正：您可在应用内查看及更新个人资料和行程数据。
  • 删除：您可在应用或 iCloud 管理界面自主删除对应数据。
  • 撤回授权：您可在设备的系统设置中调整 Apple 登录授权、iCloud 同步权限等；撤回后，部分功能可能无法继续使用。
  • 账号注销：应用内提供账号注销入口；完成注销后，我们将删除或匿名化处理与账号相关信息（法律法规另有规定的除外）。
  • 投诉与申诉：如对本政策或个人信息处理存疑，可通过本政策列明的方式联系我们。

在验证您的身份后，我们将在法律规定的合理期限内处理您的请求。对于重复或不合理的请求，我们可能予以拒绝。

八、未成年人保护

我们主要面向成年人。若您未满 18 周岁，应在监护人指导下使用“墨鱼足迹”，并在提交个人信息前征得监护人同意。如我们发现未经监护人同意收集了未成年人信息，将尽快删除。

九、政策更新

我们可能不时更新本政策。我们会在应用内显著位置或以其他合理方式发布更新版本，并在生效前通知您。继续使用墨鱼足迹即表示您同意受更新政策约束。

十、联系与反馈

如您对本政策或个人信息保护有任何疑问、意见或建议，可通过以下方式与我们联系：

  • 电子邮箱：mooyucc@qq.com

  • 客服渠道：在“墨鱼足迹”应用内通过“设置 - 帮助与反馈”提交建议

我们将在收到您的反馈后 15 个工作日内答复。

感谢您信任"墨鱼足迹"。我们将持续优化隐私保护措施，为您提供安全可靠的体验。

"MooFootprint" User Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 11, 2025

I. Introduction

Thank you for using "MooFootprint". We highly value your personal information and privacy protection. This User Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal information, as well as how you can manage this information. Please read and fully understand the contents of this policy before using our products or services. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please stop using "MooFootprint" immediately.

II. Information Collection Scope

We follow the principles of legality, legitimacy, and necessity, collecting only the minimum information required for core functionality:

  • Apple Sign-In Information: When you use "Sign in with Apple", we obtain necessary information provided by Apple (which may include your name, avatar, Apple ID associated email or hidden email, etc.) based on your authorization, for account creation, login verification, and identity identification.
  • iCloud Data: After your authorization, we write and read content such as trips, destinations, and notes in your iCloud account to help you sync data across devices. This data is only stored in your personal iCloud space.
  • Device and Operation Information: Including device model, operating system, application version, etc., for basic statistics, performance optimization, and security auditing.
  • Usage Logs and Diagnostic Information: Including crash logs, error records, etc., used only to improve product experience and troubleshoot issues.

III. Information Usage

We will process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and maintaining core functionality, such as trip planning, destination recording, route management, etc.;
  • Ensuring service security, such as identity verification, anti-fraud, and preventing illegal use;
  • Providing customer support and feedback channels, responding to your inquiries and suggestions;
  • Conducting data analysis and research to improve product performance and user experience;
  • With your consent, sending you notifications, updates, activities, or personalized content;
  • Complying with laws, regulations, and regulatory requirements, such as assisting administrative or judicial authorities in lawful execution.

We will not use information for purposes unrelated to those mentioned above.

IV. Information Storage and Protection

  • Storage Location and Method: Except for logs and necessary operational information stored securely by us within China, your core data such as trips is stored in your personal iCloud account and will not be stored separately on our own servers. We only read and write this data with your authorization.
  • Storage Period: We only retain information for the period necessary to achieve the above purposes; after the period expires, service termination, or your active deletion/account cancellation, we will delete or anonymize the information (except as otherwise required by laws and regulations). Data stored in iCloud can be managed and deleted by you.
  • Security Measures: We adopt encryption for transmission and storage, access control, permission levels, audit monitoring, and other measures to protect the security of your information. Only authorized personnel can access necessary information and have signed confidentiality agreements.
  • Security Incident Handling: In the event of an information security incident, we will promptly inform you of the incident situation, impact, and remedial measures taken in accordance with legal requirements, and report to the competent authorities.

V. Information Sharing, Transfer, and Public Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information, nor do we share or transfer your information to third parties other than Apple. Sharing or transfer may only occur in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit authorization or consent;
  • In accordance with laws, regulations, administrative supervision, or judicial requirements;
  • Necessary collaboration with Apple-related ecosystem services (such as CloudKit, Sign in with Apple); these services are all subject to Apple's official privacy policy;
  • Enterprise mergers, reorganizations, and other situations. If information transfer is involved, we will require the new information holder to continue to be bound by this policy or obtain your authorization again.

Except as otherwise required by law, we will not publicly disclose your personal information. If public disclosure is indeed necessary, we will notify you in advance in a manner that complies with legal requirements and obtain your consent.

VI. Third-Party Services and SDKs

"MooFootprint" currently only uses SDKs provided by Apple, including but not limited to:

  • Sign in with Apple (for identity authentication);
  • CloudKit / iCloud (for data storage and synchronization).

These services are subject to Apple's relevant privacy policy. We recommend that you refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

VII. Your Rights and Information Management

We respect your relevant rights regarding personal information:

  • Access and Correction: You can view and update your personal profile and trip data within the application.
  • Deletion: You can independently delete corresponding data in the application or iCloud management interface.
  • Withdraw Authorization: You can adjust Apple sign-in authorization, iCloud sync permissions, etc., in your device's system settings; after withdrawal, some features may no longer be available.
  • Account Cancellation: The application provides an account cancellation entry; after cancellation is completed, we will delete or anonymize information related to the account (except as otherwise required by laws and regulations).
  • Complaints and Appeals: If you have any questions about this policy or personal information processing, you can contact us through the methods listed in this policy.

After verifying your identity, we will process your request within a reasonable period as required by law. We may refuse repeated or unreasonable requests.

VIII. Protection of Minors

We primarily target adults. If you are under 18 years old, you should use "MooFootprint" under the guidance of a guardian and obtain guardian consent before submitting personal information. If we discover that we have collected information from minors without guardian consent, we will delete it as soon as possible.
